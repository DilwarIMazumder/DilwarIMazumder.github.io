About Me

Welcome to my academic webpage. I am a Lecturer of Mathematics with interests spanning pure and applied mathematics, relativistic cosmology, machine learning, fuzzy systems. My work focuses on developing mathematical frameworks that connect theoretical foundations with practical applications in science and engineering.


Beyond my research activities, I am deeply committed to teaching, mentoring, and public education. I believe mathematics should be accessible to everyone and strive to inspire students through innovative teaching methods and interdisciplinary learning.
